Chicxulub Crater
The impact site is in the Yucatán Peninsula, Mexico.
The crater is about 180 km (110 miles) wide and 20 km (12 miles) deep.
The asteroid was estimated to be 10–15 km (6–9 miles) wide.


Effects of the Impact
Massive explosion (equivalent to billions of nuclear bombs).
Wildfires, earthquakes, and mega tsunamis.
Dust and debris blocked sunlight, causing a global winter.
75% of life on Earth went extinct, including most dinosaurs (except birds).
