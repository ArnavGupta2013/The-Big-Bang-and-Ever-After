Types of Dinosaurs



1. Theropod
(Meat-eating, bipedal dinosaurs) Tyrannosaurus rex � The famous apex predator with a powerful bite


2. Sauropod
(Long-necked, plant-eating dinosaurs) Apatosaurs � A giant, giraffe-like dinosaur with long front legs


3. Ornithopod
(Bipedal/herbivorous dinosaurs, often had duckbills) Iguanodon � Early plant-eater with thumb spikes for defense.


4. Ceratopsian
(Horned dinosaurs with frills) Triceratops � Three-horned dinosaur that lived alongside T. rex.


5. Ankylosaur
(Armored dinosaurs with body plates and clubs) Ankylosaurus � A heavily armored dinosaur with a clubbed tail.


6. Pachycephalosaur
(Dome-headed, thick-skulled dinosaurs) Pachycephalosaurus � Thick-headed dinosaur that might have used headbutting in fights.


7. Dromaeosaur
(Raptor-like, small but fast and intelligent predators) Velociraptor � Fast, feathered predator with a deadly toe claw.


8. Archosaurs
It is a super group that includes all Dinosaurs.
