DINOSAURS
� The 'Age of Dinosaurs' (the Mesozoic Era) included three consecutive geologic time periods.
� THE TRIASSIC, JURASSIC, CRETACEOUS PERIODS.
� The current descendants of dinosaurs are BIRDS AND REPTILES.
� The four mostly known dinosaurs are ARCHOSURS, TYRANNOSAURUS REX, TRICERATOPS and APATOSAURUS.




THE MIGHTY TYRANNOSAURUS REX
� It is known as the king of dinosaurs.
� Its closest DNA matches a chicken and ostrich.
� It was a carnivore.
� It lived in present day NORTH AMERICA.
� It belongs to the CRETACEOUS PERIOD.




THE TRICERATOPS
� It�s known as triceratops because it had three horns.
� It was an herbivore.
� Its natural predator was the T-rex.
� It belongs to the CRETACEOUS PERIOD.
� It lived in present day NORTH AMERICA.




THE APATOSAURUS
� It was a sauropod meaning long necked and tailed.
� It was an herbivore.
� It lived in present day NORTH AMERICA.
� It belongs to the JURASSIC PERIOD.
