What Are Mammals?? Their Types




1. Placental Mammals
The largest group, where babies develop inside the mother’s body and get nutrients through a placenta.
Examples: Humans, lions, elephants, whales, bats, and dogs


2. Marsupials
These mammals give birth to tiny, underdeveloped babies that continue growing in a pouch.
Examples: Kangaroos, koalas, opossums, and wallabies


3. Monotremes
A rare group of egg-laying mammals.
Examples: Platypus and echidna
